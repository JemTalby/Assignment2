Who is this Website For?

This website is for those teenagers looking to make a positive change in their lives. Physical activity is great for your health and a great way for you to make friends and develop social skills. Also as you get older, you'll notice how much more important your diet becomes. By browsing this website you will find inforation on:

  • Physical Activity, and how you can incorporate it into your life
  • How to spot sedentary behaviours and prevent them
  • The importance of nutrition
  • Varieties of simple meals and drinks you can make yourself at home

Why you Should Read on

You can gain alot from looking and reading the content in this website. By making simple changes to your diet and lifestyle you can make a huge difference to both your short term and long term health. The idea is not to make a massive change to your lifestyle, but a gradual one. Find out for yourself by taking on the informaton, and incorporating some of the ideas into your life. I guarantee you will see the difference. If you don't, your body will.

Three Tips for the Week

Build Slowly

Start with small steps, not big ones straight away. Try a simple 10 minute walk after school, and then build it up by a minute each session. If you want you could also increase the intensiy of your walking. You'll see that the added improvement leads to increased motivation.

Limit Screen time

Try not to spend more then 2 hours a day watching TV or playing video games. Try incorporating some activites during adverts, such as situps or pushups. You can even schedule activity breaks inbetween TV and gaming sessions.

Start Strength Training

You don't even need to join a gym to do strength training. Just by simply doing pushups, crunches, lifting weights or exercises with resistance bands you can significanly increase your strength. This will have a significant effect on lowering body fat percentage aswell as increase muscle, strength and power. I guarantee you will see the difference.

Jeremy Talbot; 2015; Edith Cowan University
