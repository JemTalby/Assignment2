Light meals

Light meals include breakfast, sandwiches and soups. They are usually very quick and easy to prepare. They provide a less substantial contribution to daily intake of energy (kilojoules) and fat. Click on the picture and it will take you to the recipe.

Jacket Potatoes

Snacks

Snacks include sweet or savoury, cakes, biscuits, muffins and breads, dips and sandwiches. They feature fruits or vegetables and have less than half the kilojoules of light meal dishes. Click on the picture and it will take you to the recipe.

Fruity Ice Treats

Drinks

Drinks containing blended fruits and vegetables are a nutritious snack. They contain about half the kilojoules of light meal dishes. Click on the picture and it will take you to the recipe.

Apricot Lassi

Desserts

Desserts include hot or cold sweet dishes usually eaten after a meal and bakery items. They provide fruit and are lower in energy (kilojoules), added sugar and fats than traditional sweet recipes. Click on the picture and it will take you to the recipe.
